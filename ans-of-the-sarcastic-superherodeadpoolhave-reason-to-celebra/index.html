Fans of the sarcastic superheroDeadpoolhave reason to celebrate asRyan Reynoldsis apparently back on board to play the title character.

Heroic Hollywoodis reporting that Reynolds, who ledDeadpoolto a global box office of more than $750 million, will reprise the Marvel character for the already greenlit sequel. Considering the first film’s massive haul, this news really isn’t a surprise. It was simply just a matter of what kind of raise Reynolds would see since he only received areported$2 million upfront salary the first time around.

Since its February 2016 release,Deadpoolhas smashed box office records and is already the highest grossing R-rated movie ever, surpassingThe Matrix Reloaded. Unlike other recent superhero movies (cough…BvS…cough), it also was critically lauded, with a rating on Rotten Tomatoes of 83 percent.

There is no release date yet for Deadpool 2.
